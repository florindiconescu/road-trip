Trip Essentials

What you need to prepare before departure

Vehicle Preparation

  • Full vehicle service and inspection
  • Check tire condition and pressure
  • Verify camper van equipment functionality
  • Pack emergency repair kit
  • Ensure valid vehicle registration
  • Check spare tire and tools

Insurance & Coverage

  • European car insurance (Green Card)
  • Travel insurance for all passengers
  • Camper van specific insurance
  • Breakdown coverage (ADAC, AAA equivalent)
  • Health insurance cards (EHIC)
  • Emergency evacuation coverage

Navigation & Communication

  • GPS device with European maps
  • Physical road maps as backup
  • Mobile phone with international roaming
  • Local SIM cards (if needed)
  • Emergency contact list
  • Translation apps downloaded

Money & Banking

  • Euros for all countries
  • Swiss Francs for Switzerland
  • Credit/debit cards with no foreign fees
  • Notify banks of travel plans
  • Emergency cash reserves
  • Digital payment apps setup

Camping & Accommodation

  • Research camping sites along route
  • Book campsites in advance (especially Cannes)
  • Understand wild camping regulations
  • Backup hotel options
  • Camping apps (Park4Night, Camper Contact)
  • Camping membership cards

Connectivity & Apps

  • Google Maps offline downloads
  • Weather apps
  • Currency converter
  • Language translation apps
  • Camping and travel apps
  • Emergency services apps
